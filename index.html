Frequently asked questions

  • What user interactions are available?
    The audience is able to communicate via group chat and send questions to the speakers, which can be displayed by the host with a click of a button, on the screen during the QaA section of the event.
  • How can I ensure show-up rates will be high?
    There are multiple ways to ensure that registrants will show up at your events. Our imbedded notification and event promotion tool will send automated emails and reminders. For VIP guests we can offer premium treats that increase engagement.
  • What software do I need to download?
    WebinARE platform is a web-based technology that does not require you to  download any software neither on the audience or the speakers side. The only thing is required is a web browser.
  • Can the sessions be recorded?
    All the live sessions are recorded and made available for Video On Demand in the event page, so you can create content that will live after life sessions have ended. Your audience can access and replay with the same registration credentials.
  • How difficult is it to setup and facilitate a live event?
    WebinARE provides a fully-managed service, train your speakers and facilitates the live broadcast. Based on your basic event information and data, every aspect or the setup will be handled while you will have access to an admin portal interface to make changes.
